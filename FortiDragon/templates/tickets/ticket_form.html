How to Write a Good Ticket
  • Title: Use a short summary, like “Cannot log in to FortiDragon” or “Printer in Lab 3 offline”.
  • Description: Include what you were doing, any error messages, and what you already tried.
  • Priority:
    • Low: Minor issue or question.
    • Medium: Problem but there is a workaround.
    • High: Blocking your work.
    • Urgent: System-wide or time-critical issue.

Clear tickets help staff resolve your issue faster and keep the queue organized.
